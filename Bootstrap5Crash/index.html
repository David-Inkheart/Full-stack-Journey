Collaborate with us

Hey there book lovers and tech enthusiasts! Are you looking for an opportunity to use your passions or skills to make a difference in the world of literature? Look no further than our Open Source BookHub project! We are a group of passionate developers dedicated to creating a platform for readers to write, teach what they know, connect, discuss, and share their favorite books.

Lovers of knowledge, we believe that everyone should have access to great literature, regardless of their location or financial situation. By contributing to our project, you will be helping to make this vision a reality. Also for the developers, whether experienced or just starting out, if there is something you can help us do better, please, let us know. We value diversity and welcome contributions from people of all backgrounds and skill levels. So if you have a love for books and a desire to make a positive impact on the world, pitch in today!

Frequently Asked Questions

We aim to reach the whole world and people from all walks of life, whether they be students, educators, professionals, researchers, passionate or leisure readers .

  • Cost

    OS|BH can provide free access to a wide range of materials on a variety of topics, which can help to reduce financial barriers to learning and exploration. One of the primary problems that OS|BH aims to solve the high cost of educational resources, which can be a significant barrier for individuals and institutions with limited financial resources. Thus, it will engender access to information and educational resources by providing free and open access to a vast collection of digital books, articles, tutorials, journals, and other learning materials.

    Also, traditional books and resources related to hobbies and passions can be expensive, which can limit access to these materials for people with limited financial resources. We hope that this makes it easier to engage in leisure readings or studies.

  • Limited Access

    People who are interested in niche topics or hobbies may struggle to find relevant materials in traditional libraries or bookstores. Our Open Source E-library can provide access to a wide range of materials on these topics, regardless of their popularity or mainstream appeal.

  • Geographic Constraints

    People who live in remote or rural areas may struggle to access materials related to their interests or hobbies. OS|BH can provide access to materials regardless of geographic location, as long as the individual has access to the internet.

  • Outdated Materials

    Some books or other resources related to hobbies or passions may be out of print or otherwise unavailable in traditional libraries or bookstores. OS|BH can provide access to materials that may be difficult or impossible to find elsewhere, helping to preserve knowledge and learning related to niche topics

  • Overall, OSBH can be a valuable resource for people who are interested in learning more about niche topics or pursuing hobbies and passions, providing access to a wide range of materials regardless of geographic location, financial resources, or language barriers.
